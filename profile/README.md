## 🌍 Who we are

We build **payment infrastructure that lives at the edge** — where the shopper,
the shelf, and the gate actually are. We pair frontier cryptography with
real-world retail hardware and a deep respect for Turkish financial regulation.

Our north star: **let people spend the future value of their money today, prove
who they are with their face or their phone, and walk out — without ever handing
their biometric data to a cloud.**

---

## 🧠 What we care about

- **Privacy by cryptography, not policy** — Fully Homomorphic Encryption means
  biometric data is *never* stored in reconstructable form. Zero-knowledge, by
  design.
- **Compliance as architecture** — KVKK (Law 6698), Law 6493, Law 5651, and BRSA
  Art. 34 are baked into the system, not bolted on.
- **Partner-first** — we extend **Moka United's** wallet, card-issuing, and POS
  ecosystem rather than competing with it. More volume, stickier deposits,
  Face-ID SoftPOS.
- **Edge over cloud** — store-local Wi-Fi Direct meshes keep data in-country and
  cut OPEX.
